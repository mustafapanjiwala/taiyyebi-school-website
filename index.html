Children's Day

Children’s day is celebrated on 14th November every year to commemorate the birth anniversary of Jawaharlal Nehru, who was also called Chacha Nehru, due to his love for children. The highlights of the day was of giving love, attention, and affection to children.

Drawing Competion

A drawing competition was arranged for the students of TEMHSS. The students showed their active part in this program. It helped them to be aware of various colors in paintings.

Zakereen Workshop

TEMHSS - Zakereen Workshop conducted on the auspicious day of Milad Mubarak Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a.
Janab M Shabbir bhai Boriwala, Zakir e Husain a.s. since many years, currently doing khidmat as Headmoallim in Amakin Burhaniyah, Bangalore shared some great tips to our young zakereen.
