About Us

An ideal education is one where spiritual and material knowledge and understandings are seamlessly integrated. The curriculum, which has been designed in the light of the Quranand Shariat, address all the developmental areas that make up the ‘whole child’- including their social, cognitive, physical, emotional, and spiritual being. Al Dail Ajal AlMuqaddas His Holiness Dr. Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin r.a. states: “One shouldnot just transfer knowledge but through it (knowledge) transform a child”.

Taiyebi English Higher Secondary School aims in achieving all-round development of a child and thrives for it through continues collaboration between all its stakeholders.In the Saya Mubarak of His HolinessDr. Syedna Mufaddal Saifuddin t.u.s. and his Raza Mubarak each student isl earning by building 21st century skills, attitudes and conceptual understanding.TEMHSS is blessed by Syedna’s innumerable bounties among which is the newly constructed building at Mufaddal Park, equipped with all the amenities and facilities needed for an all-round development of a child.

VISION

Education should be imparted from a Correct Islamic Standpoint and Perspective. Its target is to become a Mumin Mukhlis(a loving, compassionate person of faith). Love and Compassion for Aale Mohammed AS is his ultimate goal. Eternity and Salvation is the fruit and essence of education.

MISSION

The aim to develop Takamul-e-Shakhsi, a well-rounded personality in each child
